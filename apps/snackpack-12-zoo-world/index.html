Features

  • Animal zones: Safari, ocean, jungle, farm, arctic, Australia, and night animals.
  • Zoo Exhibit: a passport-style visit for every animal with habitat hotspots, compare stats, a timeline, and a stamp-earning quiz.
  • Learning depth: animal details, daily facts, bundled image galleries, conservation profiles, and story books.
  • Mini-games: sound quiz, habitat match, memory cards, diet sorter, and more.
  • Progression: stickers, badges, visit streaks, neutral family setup, and local progress tracking.

Family-friendly design

Zoo World is built for learners ages 6-15 with no account, no third-party ads, local progress storage, a neutral family setup gate, and grown-up controls placed behind a parental gate. The whole zoo unlocks with a single one-time purchase — no subscriptions, ever.

Privacy-first learning

Bundled animal content

Zoo World includes curated animal, story, gallery, and conservation images in the app bundle so core learning content is available without account sign-in or gameplay syncing.
