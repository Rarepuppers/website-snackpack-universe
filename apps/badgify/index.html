Certificate & award maker

Every kind of award, from the office to the group chat.

Badgify is built for the certificate you actually need right now — Employee of the Month, a class award, a gaming MVP trophy, a meme-worthy Rage Quit Award, or a holiday Secret Santa certificate. Pick a template, fill in a name and an award line, and export.

  • 118 templates across 10 categories, with free templates to try right away in every category.
  • Favorites and recent history so your go-to templates are one tap away.
  • PNG and PDF export, ready to print or share.
  • Batch generate a whole class or team list into one PDF, no retyping (Pro).
  • Lifetime Pro ($9.99, one-time) unlocks every template and removes the watermark — no subscription.

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about Badgify.

Is Badgify free?

Yes. Badgify is free to download, and a set of free templates in every category are watermarked but usable right away. Lifetime Pro ($9.99, one-time) unlocks every template and removes the watermark everywhere, or you can remove the watermark on a single certificate for $2.99.

What platforms is Badgify available on?

It's available now on Android via Google Play. An iOS version is planned for the future.

What kinds of certificates can I make?

118 templates across 10 categories: Gag & Meme, Workplace, Pets, Tech & Dev Culture, Gaming, Family & Friends, Adulting & Life Admin, Academic & School, Sports & Race, and Holiday & Seasonal.

Is Badgify a subscription?

No. Lifetime Pro is a one-time payment that unlocks everything forever — there is no subscription.

Who makes Badgify?

Badgify is made by SnackPack Studios, part of the SnackPack Universe lineup of calm, family-friendly apps.
