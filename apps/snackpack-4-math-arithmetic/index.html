Basic Math

A SnackPack math app focused on arithmetic practice — learn, practise, and play across addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, and more. The page is prepared for store copy, launch links, and final screenshots.

Learn · Practise · Play

Seventeen topics, from number bonds to written methods.

Basic Math walks kids through core arithmetic one topic at a time — with riddles, mixed challenges, daily and boss challenges layered on top to keep practice fresh.

  • Seventeen topics: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, Number Bonds, Equal Groups, Skip Counting, Place Value, Fractions, Measurement, Time, Money, Decimals, Written Methods, Geometry, Riddles, and Mixed Challenge.
  • Daily challenges, boss challenges, and word-problem practice.
  • The same SnackPack characters and visual world as the rest of the series.
