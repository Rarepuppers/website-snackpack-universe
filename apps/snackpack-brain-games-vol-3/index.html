A third set of classics

Twenty-three more games, the same calm, ad-free feel.

Brain Games Vol 3 picks up where the first two volumes left off — but leans into board strategy and pen-and-paper logic. Play a full game of Chess or Backgammon, line up Five in a Row, then unwind with Killer Sudoku, a Slitherlink loop, or a hand of Spider Solitaire. Like every SnackPack release, it's built for people who just want to sit down and play: no ads, no sign-in, no connection required.

  • Board strategy: Chess, Chess Puzzles, Draughts, Five in a Row and Backgammon.
  • Logic puzzles: Killer Sudoku, KenKen, Bridges, Slitherlink, Akari, Nurikabe, Masyu and more.
  • Plays fully offline once installed — perfect for flights and commutes.
  • Best scores, win rates and times saved locally on your device. No third-party ads, no tracking SDKs.

Strategy, front and centre

Vol 3 leads with the games people ask for most — a full game of Chess with four difficulty levels, Draughts, Five in a Row and Backgammon — plus a daily dose of one-move Chess Puzzles. Play against the device, fully offline, with your win rate kept on your phone.

Calm by design

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about Brain Games Vol 3.

Is Snackpack Brain Games Vol 3 out yet?

Not quite — it's in final preparation and coming soon to Google Play. This page and the privacy policy are live now.

What games are in Vol 3?

Twenty-three games: Chess, Chess Puzzles, Draughts, Five in a Row, Backgammon, Snackle, Block Blast, Sokoban, Bubble Pop, Pyramid, Killer Sudoku, Bridges, KenKen, Spider Solitaire, Flow Links, Akari, Nurikabe, Masyu, Shikaku, Hitori, Battleships, Slitherlink and Tents & Trees.

Will it have ads or tracking?

No. Like every SnackPack app, Vol 3 has no third-party ads and no tracking SDKs, and every game plays fully offline with scores saved locally on your device.

How is it different from Brain Games Vol 1 and Vol 2?

Vol 1 is thirteen classics — Sudoku, Kakuro, Solitaire, Mahjong and more. Vol 2 leans into arcade, logic and word games. Vol 3 is headlined by board strategy — Chess, Draughts, Five in a Row and Backgammon — alongside a deep set of pen-and-paper logic puzzles. See Vol 1 → See Vol 2 →
