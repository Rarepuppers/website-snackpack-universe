Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about SnackPack 123.

Is SnackPack 123 free?

Yes. It's free to download on Google Play, with no ads and no in-app purchases — just learning and play.

What ages is it for?

It's designed for young learners ages 3–7 building early number sense: counting from 1 to 20, recognising numerals, and connecting numbers to real quantities.

Does it have ads?

No. There are no ads, no dark patterns, and no in-app purchases. The design is deliberately calm and distraction-free.

What platforms is it available on?

It's available now on Android via Google Play, optimised for phones and tablets. An iOS release is planned for a later date.

Who makes SnackPack 123?

It's made by SnackPack Studios, part of the SnackPack Universe — a connected lineup of calm, family-friendly apps sharing the same characters. SnackPack ABC: Learn to Read starts the series.
