Garden World

A calm nature learning app where kids grow plants, meet pollinators and garden creatures, explore habitats, play mini-games, and build a living garden while learning real ecology.

Privacy policy Back to all apps
Nature learning

Plant, observe, discover, and grow a living habitat.

Garden World turns a garden into a science playground: flower patches, veggie beds, butterfly meadows, rainforest corners, pond life, and bee-friendly planting all connect through missions, badges, and kid-friendly facts.

  • Grow plants and unlock habitats across a connected garden map.
  • Learn pollination, life cycles, food chains, habitats, compost, and seasonal change.
  • Play mini-games including Match Seed to Plant, Pollination Puzzle, Harvest Counting, Bug Spotter, Garden Detective, and Life Cycle Order.
  • Local-first play with bundled Garden Lens images, no ads, no tracking, and a grown-ups area behind a simple gate.

Planned features

  • Garden map: area overview, daily facts, habitat progress, and mission prompts.
  • Plant and bug discovery: learn what grows where and which creatures each habitat supports.
  • Mini-games: six science activities tied to garden progress.
  • Garden Lens: close-up nature references for flowers, food plants, creatures, and habitats.
  • Grown-ups area: parent-facing learning summary and real-world activity ideas.
