First letters, first words, first books.

SnackPack ABC is built for young learners taking their first steps into reading. The alphabet comes alive through the SnackPack characters, with each letter getting its own story, sounds, and activities — all in a calm, distraction-free design built for young focus spans.

  • Learn all 26 letters through character-led stories and activities.
  • Read along with mini books featuring the SnackPack crew.
  • A bookshelf that fills up as children progress, with tracking for parents.

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about SnackPack ABC.

Is SnackPack ABC free?

Yes. It's free to download on Google Play with no ads. An optional one-time purchase unlocks the full library — no subscriptions and no pressure.

What ages is it for?

It's designed for young learners ages 3–7 taking their first steps into letters and reading, with simple navigation a small child can manage.

Does it have ads?

No. There are no ads and no pop-ups. The only option to spend is a single one-time purchase that unlocks the full library — never required. The design is deliberately calm and distraction-free, built for young focus spans.

What platforms is it available on?

It's available now on Android via Google Play, optimised for phones and tablets. An iOS release is planned for a later date.

Who makes SnackPack ABC?

It's made by SnackPack Studios, part of the SnackPack Universe — a connected lineup of calm, family-friendly apps sharing the same characters. SnackPack 123: Learn to Count continues the series.
