Brain Games

Sudoku, Solitaire, and classic puzzles in one calm, offline app. Snackpack Brain Games brings together the puzzle and card games you already love — with shared SnackPack themes and sound, no ads, and no tracking.

Privacy policyBack to all apps

What's inside

  • Puzzles: Sudoku, Minesweeper, 2048, and Word Search.
  • Cards & strategy: Solitaire, with Checkers, Connect 4, and Reversi on the way.
  • Plays fully offline: no connection needed once installed.
  • Best scores & times: your stats are tracked locally on your device.
  • Themes & sound: switch colour schemes and toggle sound effects.

Calm by design

Like every SnackPack release, Brain Games is built to be focused and respectful of your attention: no ads, no third-party tracking, and no account required. An optional Pro upgrade unlocks the full library, with billing handled securely by Google Play or the App Store.
