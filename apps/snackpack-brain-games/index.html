Brain Games

Sudoku, Solitaire, and classic puzzles in one calm, offline app. Snackpack Brain Games brings together puzzles, card games, and strategy games with shared SnackPack themes, local stats, no ads, and no tracking.

Privacy policy Back to all apps

What's inside

  • Puzzles: Sudoku, Minesweeper, 2048, and Word Search.
  • Cards and strategy: Solitaire, Checkers, Connect 4, and Reversi.
  • Arcade: Cascade, a falling-block score challenge.
  • Plays fully offline: no connection needed once installed.
  • Best scores and times: your stats, wins, and daily streak are tracked locally on your device.
  • Themes, sound, and accessibility: switch colour schemes, toggle sound effects, use larger game text, and enable colorblind mode.

Helpful play

Every game includes how-to-play rules and in-game help. Sudoku includes notes and tracked hints, Word Search supports drag or tap-start/tap-end selection, and Minesweeper includes extra lives, chord reveal, question marks, safe marks, number highlights, and optional colour flags.

Privacy-first setup

Brain Games is positioned as a mixed-audience app for children, teens, and adults. It uses a neutral age screen before app content, stores the age response locally, shows no ads, does not use AD_ID, and keeps under-13 purchase and restore options behind a grown-up gate.

Calm by design

Like every SnackPack release, Brain Games is built to be focused and respectful of your attention: no account required, no third-party ads, and no app analytics or tracking SDKs. An optional one-time Pro upgrade unlocks additional modes and themes, with billing handled by Google Play or the App Store.
