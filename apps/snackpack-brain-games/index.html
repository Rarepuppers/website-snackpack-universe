Classic puzzle pack

Nine timeless games, one calm and offline app.

Snackpack Brain Games is built for people who just want to sit down and play — no ads interrupting a Sudoku, no sign-in before a round of Solitaire, and no connection required. Switch between puzzles, cards, and strategy whenever the mood changes, and your stats follow you across all of them.

  • Nine classics: Sudoku, Solitaire, Minesweeper, 2048, Word Search, Checkers, Connect 4, Reversi, and Cascade.
  • Plays fully offline once installed — perfect for flights, commutes, and downtime.
  • Best scores, times, wins, and a daily streak tracked locally on your device.
  • Optional one-time Pro upgrade unlocks extra modes and themes.

What's inside

  • Puzzles: Sudoku, Minesweeper, 2048, and Word Search.
  • Cards and strategy: Solitaire, Checkers, Connect 4, and Reversi.
  • Arcade: Cascade, a falling-block score challenge.
  • Plays fully offline: no connection needed once installed.
  • Best scores and times: your stats, wins, and daily streak are tracked locally on your device.
  • Themes, sound, and accessibility: switch colour schemes, toggle sound effects, use larger game text, and enable colorblind mode.

Why people love it

Brain Games isn't a noisy arcade — it's a quiet corner for your favourite classics. Every game includes how-to-play rules and in-game help: Sudoku has notes and tracked hints, Word Search supports drag or tap-start/tap-end selection, and Minesweeper adds extra lives, chord reveal, question marks, safe marks, number highlights, and optional colour flags. Pick it up for a single round or settle in for a streak — no account, no ads, no pressure.

Privacy-first setup

Brain Games is a mixed-audience app for children, teens, and adults. It uses a neutral age screen before app content, stores the age response locally, shows no ads, does not use the advertising ID, and keeps under-13 purchase and restore options behind a grown-up gate.

Calm by design

Like every SnackPack release, Brain Games is built to be focused and respectful of your attention: no account required, no third-party ads, and no app analytics or tracking SDKs. An optional one-time Pro upgrade unlocks additional modes and themes, with billing handled by Google Play.

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about Snackpack Brain Games.

Is Snackpack Brain Games free?

Yes. It's free to download on Google Play. An optional one-time Pro upgrade unlocks additional modes and themes, with billing handled by Google Play.

What games are included?

Nine classics in one app: Sudoku, Minesweeper, 2048, and Word Search for puzzles; Solitaire, Checkers, Connect 4, and Reversi for cards and strategy; plus Cascade, a falling-block arcade score challenge.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once installed, every game plays fully offline with no connection required. Your best scores, times, wins, and daily streak are saved locally on your device.

Does it have ads or tracking?

No. There are no third-party ads, no advertising ID, and no app analytics or tracking SDKs. A neutral age screen is shown first, the response is stored locally, and under-13 purchase and restore options stay behind a grown-up gate.

What platforms is it available on?

It's available now on Android via Google Play. An iOS version is planned to follow.

Who makes Snackpack Brain Games?

It's made by SnackPack Studios, part of the SnackPack Universe lineup of calm, family-friendly apps.
