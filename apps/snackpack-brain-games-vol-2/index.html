Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about Brain Games Vol 2.

Is Snackpack Brain Games Vol 2 out yet?

Not quite — it's coming soon to Google Play. In the meantime you can play several of its games free in your browser at snackpackuniverse.com/play.

What games are in Vol 2?

Flappy Snacky, Snacky Worm, Table Tennis, Snakes & Ladders, Picross, and Thirteen, plus Asteroid Destroyer and a themed Crossword to follow.

Will it have ads or tracking?

No. Like every SnackPack app, Vol 2 has no third-party ads and no tracking SDKs, and every game plays fully offline with scores saved locally on your device.

How is it different from Brain Games Vol 1?

Vol 1 is the original nine classics — Sudoku, Solitaire, Minesweeper and more. Vol 2 is a fresh set led by arcade and word games like Flappy Snacky, Snacky Worm and Crossword. See Vol 1 →
