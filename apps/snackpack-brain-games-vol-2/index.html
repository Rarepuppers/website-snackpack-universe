Twenty-three more games, the same calm, ad-free feel.

Brain Games Vol 2 picks up where the first volume left off — but leans into arcade, logic and word games. Flap through pipes, grow a hungry worm, rally a game of table tennis, crack a logic-grid mystery, sort water, solve SnackWords, roll for Yahtzee, or settle in with a themed crossword. Like every SnackPack release, it's built for people who just want to sit down and play: no ads, no sign-in, no connection required.

Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about Brain Games Vol 2.

Is Snackpack Brain Games Vol 2 out yet?

Yes — it's live on Google Play. You can also play several of its games free in your browser at snackpackuniverse.com/play.

What games are in Vol 2?

Twenty-three games: Flappy Snacky, Snacky Worm, Table Tennis, Snakes & Ladders, Picross, Thirteen, Tile Slide, Logic Grid Mysteries, Inequality Grid, Sudoku X, SnackWords, Asteroid Destroyer, Brick Breaker, Water Sort, Simon, Match 3, Star Battle, Binairo, Crossword, Ludo, Yahtzee, Golf Solitaire, and Rummy 500.

Will it have ads or tracking?

No. Like every SnackPack app, Vol 2 has no third-party ads and no tracking SDKs, and every game plays fully offline with scores saved locally on your device.

How is it different from Brain Games Vol 1?

Vol 1 is twenty-four classics — Sudoku, Kakuro, Solitaire, Mahjong, Hearts, Mancala and more. Vol 2 is a fresh set led by arcade, logic and word games like Flappy Snacky, Snacky Worm, Logic Grid Mysteries, SnackWords and Crossword, plus Ludo, Yahtzee, Golf Solitaire, and Rummy 500. See Vol 1 →
