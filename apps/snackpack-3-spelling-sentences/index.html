What's inside

Spelling, sentences, and stories in one app.

Sentences & Spelling guides young learners from single words to full sentences — eight mini-games, themed word packs, and a growing bookshelf of stories to read along with. Like every SnackPack release, it's calm by design: no ads, no sign-in, no tracking.

  • Eight mini-games: Spelling Bee, Sentence Builder, Word Match, Fill in the Blank, Word Scramble, Riddle Time, Character Quiz, and Story Quiz.
  • Themed word packs that build spelling and vocabulary.
  • A bookshelf of mini stories starring the SnackPack crew.
  • The same characters and visual world as the rest of the series.
