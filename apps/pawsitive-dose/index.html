Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers about Pawsitive Dose.

Is Pawsitive Dose free?

Yes. Pawsitive Dose is free to download on Google Play. Optional Pro features unlock more daily cards, extra archive history, photo downloads, and supporter perks.

What platforms is Pawsitive Dose available on?

It's available now on Android via Google Play. An iOS version is planned for the future.

What does Pawsitive Dose do?

It delivers a gentle daily mood boost: curated animal photos, affirmations, uplifting quotes, mood check-ins, a reviewed Daily Pawtrait, saved cards and folders, twice-daily reminders, and Android home-screen widgets.

Does Pawsitive Dose have ads or social feeds?

No. Pawsitive Dose is built as a calm, low-pressure reset — no endless social feed and no doomscrolling, just a quick, wholesome lift whenever you need one.

Who makes Pawsitive Dose?

Pawsitive Dose is made by SnackPack Studios, part of the SnackPack Universe lineup of calm, family-friendly apps.
