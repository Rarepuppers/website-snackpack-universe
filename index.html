About the Studio

The SnackPack Universe

SnackPack Studios is building a connected world of learning apps anchored by the same cast of characters — from the cheerful corgi peeking out of the backpack to the friendly bear at the picnic. Every app lives in the same universe, shares the same art style, and is built with the same care.

Ten apps are planned. The first two are ready for launch on Google Play. The rest are in the pipeline — each one a new subject, a new adventure, and the same beloved world.
