How to play Goalkeeper Hero

You face 12 shots. Before each kick, the striker leans toward a likely side. Choose left, centre, or right before the timer runs down. If your dive matches the shot, you make the save and score points. Quick saves and long streaks are worth more.

Rookie mode gives clearer tells and more time. Pro and Legend shrink the reaction window, hide some tells, and make the striker less predictable.

Tips to save more shots

  • Watch the striker's lean, not just the ball.
  • Do not always chase the obvious tell. Harder strikers fake more often.
  • Commit quickly when you are confident. Quick saves add bonus points.
  • Protect the streak. A steady run of saves beats risky guessing.
