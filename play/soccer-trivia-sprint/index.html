How to play Soccer Trivia Sprint

You get 12 soccer trivia questions. Choose the correct answer before the timer runs out. Correct answers score points, fast answers add a speed bonus, and streaks add even more. A wrong answer or timeout resets your streak.

Rookie mode has more time and easier questions. Pro and Legend reduce the timer and include tougher questions about rules, tournaments, positions, and football culture.

Tips for a higher score

  • Answer easy questions quickly to build early streak points.
  • Read the whole question before tapping: some answers are close.
  • On Legend, skip overthinking and trust your first read.
  • Replay to learn the question pool and improve your sprint time.
