How to play Flag Frenzy

Every day, Flag Frenzy picks one of the 48 teams at the World Cup 2026. You have 6 guesses to find it. Type any team name into the box — a dropdown will help with spelling. Each guess adds a row to your guess history showing four clues compared against the mystery team: confederation, group, group-stage points, and whether the team is still alive in the tournament or already eliminated. A green chip means that clue matches the answer exactly. The mystery flag also gets a little clearer with every guess, so between the clues and the flag you should be able to close in fast.

Reading the clues

  • Confederation — green if the guessed team plays in the same confederation as the answer (UEFA, CONMEBOL, CONCACAF, CAF, AFC, or OFC).
  • Group — green if they were drawn into the same group.
  • Points — shows the guessed team's group-stage points, with an arrow pointing toward whether the answer scored more or fewer.
  • Status — green if both teams share the same still-in-it or eliminated status right now.

About Flag Frenzy

Flag Frenzy is an original SnackPack Studios daily puzzle built for the 2026 football summer, in the spirit of daily word and sports guessing games. Status and points clues update automatically as the real tournament results come in, so the game stays accurate from the group stage through the final. Try a practice round any time for extra reps that don't affect your daily streak.

Frequently asked questions

Is Flag Frenzy free?

Yes. It plays free in your browser with no ads, no sign-in, and nothing to install.

Is there a new puzzle every day?

Yes. Everyone gets the same mystery team each day, and your progress for the day is saved if you come back later.

What happens if I run out of guesses?

The answer is revealed and your streak resets to zero. Come back tomorrow for a new team, or try a practice round any time.

Is this an official World Cup game?

No. It is an original SnackPack Studios soccer game and is not affiliated with FIFA, the World Cup, any team, league, or governing body.
