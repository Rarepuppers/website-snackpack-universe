How to play Flappy Snacky

Flappy Snacky is a one-button arcade game. Snacky is constantly pulled down by gravity, so you tap, click, or press the space bar to make him flap upward. Each tap gives a quick lift; let go and he falls again. The whole game is finding the rhythm of taps that keeps Snacky level as pipes scroll toward you from the right. Steer him through the gap in each pair of pipes — every pair you clear scores a point. Touch a pipe, the ground, or the ceiling and the run ends. It's simple to pick up and very hard to put down.

Tips for a higher score

  • Tap little and often. Lots of small taps keep Snacky steady; big panicked taps send him into the ceiling.
  • Aim for the lower third of each gap. It leaves room to flap up, which is easier to control than falling fast.
  • Find a rhythm. The pipes are evenly spaced — settle into a steady tap-tap-tap rather than reacting late.
  • Look ahead, not at Snacky. Watch the next gap coming in, not the bird itself.
  • Try Classic first. Hard mode is faster with tighter gaps; Night mode is the same speed as Classic with a darker sky.

Frequently asked questions

Is Flappy Snacky free?

Yes — it plays free in your browser with no ads, no sign-in, and nothing to install.

What are the controls?

Tap or click the board, or press the space bar or up arrow, to flap. That's the only control.

What's the difference between the modes?

Classic is the standard speed and gap size. Hard is faster with tighter gaps. Night uses Classic rules with a dark sky.
