How to play Keepy-Uppy

Tap or click the ball before it hits the grass. Every good touch pops the ball back into the air and adds points. Clean centre touches keep the ball under control, while edge touches send it sideways. Collect stars for bonus points and keep the combo alive for a bigger score.

Chill mode is forgiving. Match mode adds more speed and sideways drift. Chaos mode makes the ball smaller, faster, and harder to recover.

Tips for a higher score

  • Tap the lower half of the ball to lift it cleanly.
  • Centre touches reduce drift and help preserve your combo.
  • Only chase stars when the ball is already under control.
  • Use short taps on mobile. Long presses do not help.
