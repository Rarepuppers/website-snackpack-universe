How to play Crossbar Challenge

You have 15 shots to hit the soccer crossbar. Drag the target along the bar, set your power, choose curl, and shoot. A crossbar hit scores points, and a perfect centre-bar strike scores more. Consecutive hits build a streak bonus.

Each shot changes the distance and wind. Rookie mode is forgiving. Pro and Legend make the target thinner and the wind stronger, so power control matters more.

Tips to hit the crossbar

  • Adjust power when the ball moves farther from goal.
  • Use curl to correct for side wind instead of aiming wildly off-centre.
  • A perfect hit is valuable, but a safe bar clip protects your streak.
  • Watch the wind marker before every shot. It changes often.
