**Status:** Generated, normalized, and integrated — 17 July 2026

## Final prompt set

- **Body:** exact 4 × 3 high-three-quarter final-boss sheet; colossal six-limbed fortress silhouette; three behavior rows; stable pivot; no detached attacks, eggs, tendrils, decals, UI, text, ground, or shadow.
- **Nodes:** exact 4 × 2 modular crown states; closed top row and exposed/damaged cyan neural cores bottom row; no complete boss, attacks, UI, text, or ground.
- **Effects:** exact 4 × 3 atlas in the documented order; authored accents supplement code-owned lanes, arcs, radii, collision, and safe zones.
- **Portrait:** single centered close portrait preserving the body identity for HUD and encounter banner crops; no frame, nameplate, health bar, or text.
- **Environment/vault:** exact 4 × 2 atlas; four breach states and four victory-vault states; no full arena tile, item text, currency, UI, or boss body.

All generation used a flat pure-blue chroma background. Retained chroma and transparent masters, final prompts, deterministic nearest-neighbour normalizer, runtime atlases, pivots, and manifest contracts remain versionable for later Steam packaging.
