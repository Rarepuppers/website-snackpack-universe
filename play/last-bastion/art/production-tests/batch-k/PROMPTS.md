# Batch K generation prompts

Mode: four independent generations, each using `batch-c-effect-atlas-v1.png` as the style reference and a flat `#FF00FF` chroma background. The generated originals are retained as `*-chroma.png`; transparent edit masters and the normalized runtime atlas are derived locally.

## Shared direction

Production-quality 2D pixel-art VFX for Last Bastion, matching the reference atlas's crisp chunky pixels, limited palette, luminous highlights, strong silhouettes, generous cell gutters, and gameplay readability at 48 x 48. Effects contain no creature, text, icon panel, border, cast shadow, baked timing, or gameplay geometry.

## Blaze strip

Exactly four horizontal frames of a persistent Burning/Blaze body overlay: small asymmetric red-orange flame tongues with ivory-yellow cores and rising ember flecks. Progression: low left lick, tall split flame, low right lick, broad brief flare. Preserve a fast flicker rhythm.

## Overload strip

Exactly four horizontal frames of teal-white electrical filaments crawling around an implied creature perimeter. Progression: sparse side arcs, bright branching crown, nearly dark frame with two sparks, brilliant cross-body crackle. Keep the centre open and preserve the deliberate strobe rhythm.

## Corrode strip

Exactly four horizontal frames of a lingering Toxic/Corrode overlay: sickly chartreuse dripping film around the upper and side perimeter plus a few rising gas bubbles. Avoid splash/explosion language. Preserve a slow, lazy bubble-and-drip rhythm.

## Freeze strip

Exactly three horizontal frames of pale-blue crystalline rime creeping upward from the ground contact with tiny facets and pinprick glints. Keep the body open, avoid snowflake or ice-block iconography, and preserve an almost-static shimmer rhythm.
