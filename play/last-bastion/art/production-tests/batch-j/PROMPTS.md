# Batch J prompt contract

Built-in image generation produced six flat-magenta chroma masters. The exact production prompts are preserved in the Codex task history; this file records their durable constraints.

- **Swarm Scuttler:** exact 4 × 2 south/north/east/west grid; pursuit and compressed pack-rush rows; thin coral shell, long sprinting legs, hot-yellow sensory streak; fragile/fast silhouette.
- **Razorlord:** exact 4 × 4 east-facing grid; pursuit, blade-raised wind-up, low committed dash, exposed recovery; scarred coral-charcoal crest, elongated blade limbs, violet joints, hostile-orange eye.
- **Blightspitter:** exact 4 × 3 east-facing grid; running positioning, inflated toxic wind-up, deflated recovery; oversized acid-lime gland, olive-charcoal body, coral ridges.
- **Quillback Matriarch:** exact 4 × 4 east-facing grid; positioning, crown-charge, upward launch, sparse-crown recovery; broad dark body, coral armour, ivory-coral spine crown, amber charge roots.
- **Telegraph source A v2:** exact 4 × 4; slam rings, rain reticles, clockwise fills constrained to the code-owned 120-degree sweeping sector, radial pulses, edge chevron. Source A v1 is retained but rejected because its final sweep implied a full-circle hit.
- **Telegraph source B:** exact 4 × 2; three fixed-length beam widths, spine impact, four danger-hatch variants.

All prompts required polished modern pixel art, crisp clusters, strong gameplay silhouettes, high three-quarter bodies or top-down decals, perfectly flat `#FF00FF` chroma, and no text, numbers, UI, scenery, shadows, gore, logos, or watermarks. Telegraph prompts required a hostile-warm ivory/amber/orange/coral/red palette and explicitly prohibited player teal/cyan.
