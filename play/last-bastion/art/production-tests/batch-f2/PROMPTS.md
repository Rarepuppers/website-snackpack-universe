# F2–F4 built-in image-generation prompt set

All seven images were generated with the built-in image tool on flat `#0000ff` chroma. The established Last Bastion weapon/effect/UI masters and the approved Bulwark concept were supplied as the named style or identity references.

## Bolt Carbine weapon sheet

Create an original Bolt Carbine as exactly four separate east-facing side-profile states in one evenly spaced horizontal row: idle/closed capacitor, charge and aim-settle with cyan energy gathering, fire/recoil with open accelerator rails, recover/vent with faint breech heat. Keep weapon, scale, pivot, lighting, and silhouette stable. Use charcoal machinery, warm ivory armour, aged bronze mechanisms, burgundy structure, cyan accelerator seams, and restrained orange heat. Premium crisp hand-painted pixel art; no character, hands, projectile, trail, text, UI, shadow, scenery, overlap, or divider lines.

## Bolt Carbine effects

Create an exact 4 × 2 atlas: compact cyan charge glint; east-facing ivory/cyan precision bolt; thin cyan travel wake; first-target impact with forward exit spark; penetration wake; stronger terminal second-target impact; hard-cover bronze/ivory sparks; restrained ready glint. Eight isolated 64-pixel-readable effects only; no weapon, character, enemy, gore, text, UI, shadow, scenery, overlap, or dividers.

## Bulwark Rotary Cannon weapon sheet

Preserve the approved ivory drum shroud, navy/charcoal body, orange barrel ring, six-barrel cluster, and cyan lights. Create an exact four-state east-facing row: idle, spin-up with subtle orange motor energy, firing with rotating barrel cluster and compact muzzle flare, overheated recovery with warm barrel tips. Stable scale, pivot, lighting, and proportions; no character, hands, loose projectile, long trail, text, UI, shadow, scenery, overlap, or dividers.

## Bulwark effects

Create an exact 4 × 2 atlas: six-barrel spin-up ring; short hot ivory/orange reusable ballistic tracer; compact rotary muzzle flash; brass casing; non-gory soft-target impact; blue-white armour ricochet; orange vent heat; cyan/orange ready glint. Eight isolated effects only with restrained bloom and safe padding.

## Grenade Tube weapon sheet

Design an original compact heavy break-action/revolver grenade launcher with a short ivory barrel shroud, charcoal/navy receiver, burgundy brace, bronze cylinder and hinge, cyan range light, and restrained orange chamber glow. Create an exact four-state east-facing row: idle/closed, armed aim-settle, firing with compact recoil flash, recover/reload with cylinder visibly cycling. Stable scale, pivot, lighting, and silhouette; no character, hands, loose grenade, trail, explosion, text, UI, shadow, scenery, overlap, or dividers.

## Grenade Tube effects

Create an exact 4 × 2 atlas: east-facing ivory/charcoal grenade projectile with orange fuse and cyan band; grenade travel/fuse streak; hard-surface bounce spark; warning/fuse pulse; bright compact explosion core; larger orange fire-and-smoke blast ring with charcoal fragments; cover strike; orange/cyan ready glint. Eight isolated, non-gory, 64-pixel-readable effects only.

## Weapon tile atlas

Create an exact 3 × 1 text-free motif atlas: Bolt Carbine bolt passing through two target plates; Bulwark six-barrel face with orange spin ring; Grenade Tube shell above a restrained blast star. Bold silhouettes, generous padding, readable at 36–64 pixels and beneath a 50% dark cooldown shadow. No text, bindings, cooldown wedge, timer ring, border, panel, character, shadow, scenery, overlap, or dividers.

Every prompt also required a perfectly flat, uniform `#0000ff` background with no gradient, texture, floor, cast shadow, or reflection and prohibited that key colour inside the subject.
