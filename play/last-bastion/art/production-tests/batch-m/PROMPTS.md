# Batch M prompt contract

Mode: built-in image generation. The approved Last Bastion marine/enemy pixel language and the generated family references were used as strict style guidance. All four masters requested flat `#FF00FF` chroma, chunky crisp pixel clusters, stable high-three-quarter silhouettes, no text/UI/scenery/floor shadows, and no baked gameplay geometry.

- Survivor: exact 4 × 2, one infected humanoid per cell, south/north/east/west columns, two fast-swarm gait rows.
- Marine: exact 4 × 3, guard/reposition, knife wind-up, and stagger/recovery rows, with a readable knife in wind-up cells.
- Abomination: exact 4 × 3, large composite infected humanoid, shamble/slam/recovery-collapse rows, four facings.
- Effects: exact 4 × 2, east-authored knife, throw/readiness accents, travel wake, cover/player impacts, disarm burst.

The generated magenta sources are retained unchanged; local extraction and nearest-neighbour normalization are deterministic and repeatable.
