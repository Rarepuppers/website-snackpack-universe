# Production Asset Batch F3 — Bulwark Rotary Cannon

The approved concept identity is retained across four 96-pixel ring states and an eight-frame 64-pixel effects atlas. Frames are idle, spin-up, fire, and overheated recovery; effects are spin ring, reusable ballistic tracer, muzzle flash, casing, soft-target impact, armour ricochet, vent heat, and ready glint.

Fire cadence, targeting, damage, projectile collision, and any future heat rules remain code-driven. Chroma sources and full-resolution transparent masters are retained for higher-resolution Steam rendering.
