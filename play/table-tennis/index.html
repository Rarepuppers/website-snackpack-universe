How to play Table Tennis

Table Tennis is our take on Pong, the game that started it all. A ball bounces between two paddles; you control the one on your side, sliding it up and down to keep the ball in play. Miss it and your opponent scores. The clever part is the angle: where the ball strikes your paddle decides which way it flies back, so you can aim your returns to wrong-foot the computer. First to seven points wins the match.

Controls

  • Touch / mouse — slide up and down your side of the table to move your paddle.
  • Keyboard — W and S (or the arrow keys) move your paddle.
  • 2-player — left player uses W/S, right player uses the up and down arrow keys.

Modes

  • Beginner / Club / Pro — three computer skill levels and ball speeds.
  • Turbo — the ball speeds up with every rally for a frantic finish.
  • 2 Player — pass-and-play against a friend on one device.

Can I change my paddle colour?

Yes — pick from the swatches above the table. Handy for colour preference or visibility.

How do I win?

Score by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle. The first side to reach seven points wins.
