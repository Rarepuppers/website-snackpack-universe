How to play Free Kick Curl

You have 10 free kicks. Aim inside the goal, choose your curl direction, set power, and press Shoot. A normal goal is worth 100 points. Hit a golden corner target for a bonus. Scoring several kicks in a row builds your streak and adds extra points.

The wall, keeper, and target positions change as the round goes on. On harder difficulties the wall blocks more of the shot lane and the keeper reacts faster, so you need to mix placement, power, and curl.

Tips for a higher score

  • Use curl to bend around the wall instead of shooting through it.
  • Do not overhit every shot. Too much power can send the ball high.
  • Target the golden corners when the keeper starts near the middle.
  • Keep a scoring streak alive. Streak points often decide your best score.
