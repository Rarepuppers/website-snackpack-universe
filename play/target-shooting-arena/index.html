How to play Target Shooting Arena

You get 12 shots. Tap or drag inside the goal to aim, set power, and shoot at the moving target. A normal target hit scores points. A bullseye scores more. Consecutive hits raise your combo multiplier.

Rookie mode gives larger, slower targets. Pro and Legend make targets smaller, faster, and less forgiving, so timing and power matter more.

Tips for a higher score

  • Lead the target slightly when it is moving fast.
  • Use moderate power unless you are aiming for high corners.
  • A regular hit keeps the combo alive, so do not force every bullseye.
  • Watch the ring size: smaller targets are worth more points.
