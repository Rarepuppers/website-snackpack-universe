How to play Asteroid Destroyer

Asteroid Destroyer is a modern take on the arcade classic Asteroids. Rocks drift across the screen and your job is to blow them up before they hit you. Shooting a large asteroid breaks it into two medium ones, and mediums break into fast little shards — so the screen gets busier before it clears. Destroyed rocks sometimes drop power-ups: a shield that soaks one hit, a triple-shot that widens your fire, and slow-time that eases the chaos. You have three lives; clear waves and push your high score as far as you can.

Controls

  • Keyboard — left/right arrows (or A/D) rotate, up arrow (or W) thrusts, Space fires.
  • Touch — use the on-screen rotate, thrust and fire buttons, or tap the field to shoot.

Modes

  • Classic — your ship holds position and fires straight up; time your shots as rocks close in.
  • Rotation — full Asteroids control: rotate, thrust and drift with momentum.
  • Blitz — rotation control against the clock: score as much as you can in 60 seconds, with a rising combo multiplier.
