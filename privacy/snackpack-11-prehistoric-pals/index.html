Privacy policy

Prehistoric Pals

This Privacy Policy applies to SnackPack Prehistoric Pals, a mobile app published by SnackPack Studios. It explains what information the app may handle, how that information may be used, and what choices users have.

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to SnackPack Prehistoric Pals on Android, iOS, and any later supported platforms.

Information the app may handle

  • App progress and preferences: The app may store collection progress, challenge results, badges, settings, local backup state, and similar app-state information.
  • Billing status: If premium unlock is enabled, Google Play, Apple, RevenueCat, or similar store infrastructure may process purchase status needed to unlock paid content.
  • Notifications: If daily task reminders are enabled, the app may request notification permission and store reminder preferences on the device.
  • Support contact information: If you email SnackPack Studios for support, the email address and information you include may be received and retained for support purposes.

Information the app does not currently require

  • No account required: Prehistoric Pals does not currently require users to create an account for the core app experience.
  • No ads: The intended policy position is no advertising SDK or advertising ID use.
  • No newsletter signup inside the app: Website newsletter signup is managed separately from the app experience.

How information may be used

  • To operate the app: including saving progress, preferences, premium access state, and app state.
  • To provide optional reminders: if notification features are enabled by the user.
  • To respond to support requests: if you contact SnackPack Studios directly.

Children's privacy

Prehistoric Pals may be used by children or families depending on the final store positioning. If released as child-directed or family-focused, SnackPack Studios intends to avoid knowingly collecting personal information from children except as permitted by applicable law and platform policy.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or support requests about SnackPack Prehistoric Pals, contact privacy@snackpackuniverse.com.

This policy gives Prehistoric Pals a stable privacy URL for store listings. It should be reviewed against the final shipped build before submission, especially if billing, notifications, external SDKs, analytics, or additional permissions change.
