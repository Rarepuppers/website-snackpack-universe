Privacy policy

Prehistoric Pals

This Privacy Policy applies to SnackPack: Prehistoric Pals, a mobile app published by SnackPack Studios. Prehistoric Pals is an offline dinosaur discovery and learning app where players meet prehistoric creatures, earn badges, and explore fun facts. This page explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to SnackPack: Prehistoric Pals on Android and iOS. Prehistoric Pals is a mixed-audience app that may be used by children, teens, and adults. The app uses a neutral age screen on first launch to help keep age-sensitive features, such as purchases, appropriate for each player.

Information the app handles

  • Game progress and preferences: The app stores discovered dinosaurs, earned badges, progress, statistics, selected theme, and sound settings. This information is saved locally on your device.
  • Age screen choice: The app stores the selected year of birth locally on the device to determine a broad age band. This value is not sent to SnackPack Studios.
  • Purchase status: If you buy the optional Pro upgrade, the app stores whether your device has an active entitlement so paid features stay unlocked. See "In-app purchases" below.
  • Reminder settings: If you turn on optional reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. Reminders are off unless you enable them, and no reminder content is sent to SnackPack Studios.
  • Device-level technical information: Platform providers (Google Play, Apple) may process technical data such as device type, operating system version, IP address, crash reports, or diagnostics when needed to operate their services.
  • Support contact information: If you email SnackPack Studios for support, the email address and information you include in your message may be received and retained for support purposes.

Information the app does not collect

  • No account required: Prehistoric Pals does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • No advertising: The app does not show third-party ads and does not use the device advertising identifier (AD_ID).
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs: The app does not use third-party analytics or cross-app tracking tools.
  • No microphone access: The app plays sound effects and music for output only and does not request or use the microphone.
  • No location, contacts, or photos: The app does not request access to your location, contacts, photo library, or similar personal data.

Local storage

Prehistoric Pals is primarily an offline app and relies on local, on-device storage for game state, progress, badges, preferences, reminder settings, and purchase status. This information generally remains on your device until you reset it in the app, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. The app does not sync this data to SnackPack Studios servers.

In-app purchases

Prehistoric Pals offers an optional "Pro" upgrade that unlocks additional content. Purchases are processed by the platform's billing system - Google Play Billing on Android and the App Store on iOS. Under-13 users must pass a parental gate before purchase or restore options are shown. SnackPack Studios does not receive or store your payment card details. To deliver and restore purchases, the app uses RevenueCat, a purchase-management service that validates store receipts and manages entitlements. RevenueCat may process a randomly generated app user identifier, a device identifier used for fraud prevention and to restore purchases to the correct device, together with purchase and receipt information for this purpose. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy and the store's own terms for how payment information is handled.

How information is used

  • To operate the app: including saving progress, discoveries, badges, and preferences.
  • To deliver paid features: including unlocking and restoring the Pro upgrade.
  • To deliver optional reminders: if you choose to enable them.
  • To maintain and improve the app: including fixing bugs and diagnosing crashes.
  • To respond to support requests: if you contact SnackPack Studios directly.

Children's privacy

Prehistoric Pals may be used by children as part of a mixed audience, but it does not require a child account and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app uses a neutral age screen, stores the age response locally, does not show ads, does not use tracking SDKs, and places purchase and restore options for under-13 users behind a parental gate. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support email, contact us and we will delete it.

Sharing and disclosure

SnackPack Studios does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate the app, to use standard platform services such as app distribution, crash handling, and payment or purchase-management infrastructure, to comply with legal obligations, or to respond to lawful requests.

Retention and security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to help protect information handled by the app, including the use of secure on-device storage for sensitive local values. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app and support, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Support emails may be retained for follow-up and recordkeeping.

Your choices

  • App data: You can remove all locally stored data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • Reminders: You can turn reminders on or off in the app, or in your device notification settings.
  • Purchases: You can manage purchases through your Google Play or App Store account settings.
  • Support requests: You can choose whether to contact SnackPack Studios by email.

Requesting data deletion

Prehistoric Pals relies on local, on-device storage for progress, discoveries, badges, and preferences, so uninstalling the app removes that information from your device. For the small amount of information generated off-device, email privacy@snackpackuniverse.com from the account or with the device details used with Prehistoric Pals and request deletion.

What this covers: the RevenueCat purchase/entitlement record associated with your device (product identifiers, transaction information, the app user identifier generated for purchase handling, and the device identifier used for fraud prevention and purchase restoration) will be deleted from RevenueCat within 30 days of a verified request. Google Play and App Store purchase records are retained and managed by those platforms under their own policies and cannot be deleted by SnackPack Studios. Support emails may be retained separately for recordkeeping as described above.

Platform-specific privacy requirements

Prehistoric Pals is distributed on Google Play and may also be submitted to the Apple App Store. For Apple review, the iOS build keeps its app-level privacy manifest current for required-reason APIs used by shipped dependencies such as preferences storage, secure local storage, timing, file access, and disk-space checks. The Google Play Data safety form is kept consistent with the practices described here. If platform-specific privacy practices change in future releases, this page and the related store disclosures will be updated accordingly.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The version published on this page is the current version, and the effective date will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or support requests about SnackPack: Prehistoric Pals, contact privacy@snackpackuniverse.com.
