Privacy policy

Garden World

This Privacy Policy applies to SnackPack: Garden World, a mobile app published by SnackPack Studios. Garden World is a calm, fully offline nature learning game designed for children (around ages 5-10) and families, where players grow a garden, meet plants and bugs, and earn badges. This page explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to SnackPack: Garden World on Android and iOS. Garden World is designed as a child-directed, family-friendly learning app. It runs fully offline, does not require an account, and is built to be safe for younger players.

Information the app handles

  • Game progress and preferences: The app stores garden progress, discovered plants and bugs, earned badges, selected colour scheme, and sound settings. This information is saved locally on your device.
  • Premium unlock status: Garden World includes an optional premium unlock. If it is enabled in a future release, the app stores whether your device has an active entitlement so paid features stay unlocked. See "In-app purchases" below.
  • Device-level technical information: Platform providers (Google Play, Apple) may process technical data such as device type, operating system version, IP address, crash reports, or diagnostics when needed to operate their services.
  • Support contact information: If you email SnackPack Studios for support, the email address and information you include in your message may be received and retained for support purposes.

Information the app does not collect

  • No account required: Garden World does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • No advertising: The app does not show third-party ads and does not use the device advertising identifier (AD_ID).
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs: The app does not use third-party analytics or cross-app tracking tools.
  • No network data collection: Garden World runs fully offline and does not send your gameplay data to SnackPack Studios servers.
  • No microphone access: The app plays sound effects and music for output only and does not request or use the microphone.
  • No location, contacts, or photos: The app does not request access to your location, contacts, photo library, or similar personal data.

Local storage

Garden World is an offline app and relies on local, on-device storage for game state, progress, discoveries, badges, preferences, and unlock status. This information generally remains on your device until you reset it in the app, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. The app does not sync this data to SnackPack Studios servers.

Grown-ups area

Garden World includes a parent-facing "grown-ups" area reached from Settings. It is placed behind a simple gate (a short multiplication question) that re-locks each visit, so it is not intended to be opened by young children. This area shows a learning summary computed from the child's local save and offline guidance for parents. It does not collect personal information and does not transmit data off the device.

In-app purchases

Garden World offers an optional premium unlock. In the current release, in-app billing is not enabled, so the app does not process any purchases or payment information. If billing is enabled in a future update, purchases will be handled by the platform's billing system - Google Play Billing on Android and the App Store on iOS - and purchase and restore options will be placed behind a parental gate so they are not triggered by children. SnackPack Studios does not receive or store payment card details. This policy will be updated to describe any purchase-management service used before billing goes live.

How information is used

  • To operate the app: including saving garden progress, discoveries, badges, and preferences.
  • To deliver paid features: including unlocking and restoring premium content if billing is enabled.
  • To maintain and improve the app: including fixing bugs and diagnosing crashes.
  • To respond to support requests: if you contact SnackPack Studios directly.

Children's privacy

Garden World is designed as a child-directed app for a young audience and is built to align with Google Play's Families policy and Apple's guidelines for kids' apps. It does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use the advertising identifier, and does not use analytics or cross-app tracking SDKs. Gameplay data stays on the device. Grown-up and purchase options are placed behind a parental gate. SnackPack Studios does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support email, contact us and we will delete it.

Sharing and disclosure

SnackPack Studios does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate the app, to use standard platform services such as app distribution and crash handling, to comply with legal obligations, or to respond to lawful requests.

Retention and security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to help protect information handled by the app, including the use of secure on-device storage for sensitive local values. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app and support, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Support emails may be retained for follow-up and recordkeeping.

Your choices

  • App data: You can remove all locally stored data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • Purchases: If billing is enabled, you can manage purchases through your Google Play or App Store account settings.
  • Support requests: You can choose whether to contact SnackPack Studios by email.

Platform-specific privacy requirements

Garden World is distributed on Google Play and may also be submitted to the Apple App Store. For Apple review, the iOS build keeps its app-level privacy manifest current for required-reason APIs used by shipped dependencies such as preferences storage, secure local storage, file access, and disk-space checks. The Google Play Data safety form and Families policy disclosures are kept consistent with the practices described here. If platform-specific privacy practices change in future releases, this page and the related store disclosures will be updated accordingly.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The version published on this page is the current version, and the effective date will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or support requests about SnackPack: Garden World, contact privacy@snackpackuniverse.com.
