Privacy policy

Math Arithmetic

This Privacy Policy applies to SnackPack Math Arithmetic, a mobile app published by SnackPack Studios. It explains what information the app may handle, how that information may be used, and what choices users have.

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to SnackPack Math Arithmetic on Android, iOS, and any later supported platforms. If you use this app, this page describes how information related to your use of the app may be collected, stored, processed, or shared.

Information the app may handle

  • App progress and preferences: The app may store gameplay progress, learning progress, unlocked content, settings, and similar app-state information.
  • Device-level technical information: Platform providers may process technical data such as device type, operating system version, IP address, crash reports, or diagnostics when needed to operate their services.
  • Support contact information: If you email SnackPack Studios for support, the email address and information you include in your message may be received and retained for support purposes.

Information the app does not currently require

  • No account required: SnackPack Math Arithmetic does not currently require users to create an account to use the core app experience.
  • No newsletter signup inside the app: Website newsletter signup is managed separately from the app experience.
  • No live store billing yet: Google Play and App Store billing are not yet active for the launch build. If billing is added later, this policy will be updated before release.

How information may be used

  • To operate the app: including saving progress, preferences, and app state.
  • To maintain and improve the app: including fixing bugs, diagnosing crashes, and improving stability.
  • To respond to support requests: if you contact SnackPack Studios directly.

Local storage

SnackPack Math Arithmetic is expected to rely primarily on local, on-device storage for core app state unless a future release adds optional online features. Locally stored information generally remains on your device until you remove it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

Analytics, ads, and third-party services

At launch, the intended policy position for SnackPack Math Arithmetic is that analytics, advertising SDKs, and similar third-party tracking tools are not relied on unless specifically added and disclosed. If a future version adds analytics, ads, backend syncing, notifications, or external integrations, this policy will be updated before that version is published.

Children's privacy

SnackPack Math Arithmetic may be used by children or families depending on the final app positioning. If the app is released as child-directed or family-focused, SnackPack Studios intends to avoid knowingly collecting personal information from children except as permitted by applicable law and platform policy. For iOS learning-app review, this policy should remain publicly accessible from the App Store listing and from an in-app legal or settings surface. This page should be reviewed again before store submission so the child-directed status matches the final app listing.

Sharing and disclosure

SnackPack Studios does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, or use standard platform services such as app distribution, crash handling, hosting, or payment infrastructure if those services are later enabled.

Retention and security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures may be used to help protect information handled by the app. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide support, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Support emails may be retained for follow-up and recordkeeping.

Your choices

  • App data: You can usually remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage through your device settings.
  • Support requests: You can choose whether to contact SnackPack Studios by email.
  • Future permissions: If a later version requests device permissions or optional online features, users can decide whether to grant those permissions where the platform allows it.

Platform-specific privacy requirements

SnackPack Math Arithmetic is being prepared for distribution on Google Play and may also be submitted to the Apple App Store. For Apple review, the iOS build is expected to keep its app-level privacy manifest current for any required-reason APIs used by shipped dependencies such as preferences storage, secure local storage, timing, file access, or similar system APIs. If platform-specific privacy practices differ in future releases, this page and the related store disclosures will be updated accordingly.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The version published on this page is the current version, and the effective date will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or support requests about SnackPack Math Arithmetic, contact snackpackuniverse@gmail.com.

This policy is intended to give each SnackPack app a stable privacy URL for store listings. If app features change materially, especially around billing, cloud sync, analytics, ads, accounts, notifications, or iOS privacy-manifest coverage, the policy should be reviewed and updated before release or resubmission.
