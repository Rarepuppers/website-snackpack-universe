Privacy policy

Zoo World

This Privacy Policy applies to SnackPack Zoo World, a mobile app published by SnackPack Studios. It explains what information the app may handle, how that information may be used, and what choices users have.

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to SnackPack Zoo World on Android, iOS, and any later supported platforms.

Information the app may handle

  • App progress and preferences: The app may store met animals, earned badges, mini-game scores, sticker counts, visits, streaks, theme preferences, audio preferences, and similar app-state information.
  • Local unlock status: If premium features are later enabled, the app may store unlock state locally and use standard store infrastructure for purchases.
  • Device-level technical information: Platform providers may process device type, operating system version, IP address, crash reports, or diagnostics when needed to operate their services.
  • Support contact information: If you email SnackPack Studios for support, the email address and information you include may be received and retained for support purposes.

Information the app does not currently require

  • No account required: Zoo World does not currently require users to create an account for the core app experience.
  • No ads: The intended policy position is no advertising SDK or advertising ID use.
  • No live billing yet: Billing is not currently enabled for the planned launch configuration and this policy should be updated if that changes.

How information may be used

  • To operate the app: including saving progress, preferences, badges, mini-game results, and app state.
  • To maintain and improve the app: including fixing bugs, diagnosing crashes, and improving stability.
  • To respond to support requests: if you contact SnackPack Studios directly.

Children's privacy

Zoo World may be used by children or families depending on the final store positioning. If released as child-directed or family-focused, SnackPack Studios intends to avoid knowingly collecting personal information from children except as permitted by applicable law and platform policy.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or support requests about SnackPack Zoo World, contact privacy@snackpackuniverse.com.

This policy gives Zoo World a stable privacy URL for store listings. It should be reviewed against the final shipped build before submission, especially if billing, external SDKs, analytics, notifications, cloud sync, or additional permissions are introduced.
